## Who I am

I'm more of an observer: the little things catch my eye, the ones most people just walk past. And I tend to sit with them longer than it's probably worth. Still, something keeps pulling me out the door, somewhere I haven't been yet.

## What I do

This blog exists because writing is how I sort through what I've experienced. Some of it I'd otherwise just forget. Some I want to hold onto before it fades. And some I share because keeping thoughts to myself gets old after a while.

## What else I'm into

When I'm not writing or stuck at my desk, I'm usually out and about, more some days, less others. Ideally with a camera on me. Walking or cycling out in nature is how I switch off. And at home, I cook so I don't starve: Asian food, mostly Thai, sometimes hearty German comfort food.

The quickest way to reach me is by email. You'll find the address in the [Imprint](/en/imprint).
