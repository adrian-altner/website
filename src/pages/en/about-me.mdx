## Who I Am
 
I'm the observant type – small things catch my attention that others might walk right past, and I tend to sit with them longer than most people would. At the same time, I'm always drawn back out: new places, new impressions, new questions.
 
## What I Do
 
This blog exists because writing is how I make sense of what I experience. Some of it I'd otherwise forget, some I want to capture before it fades, and some I simply share – it's better than keeping thoughts to myself.
